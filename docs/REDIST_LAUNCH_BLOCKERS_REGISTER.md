## Pre-Launch Priority List

1. Complete first supplier-recipient real transfer.
2. Capture certificate, feedback, and issue evidence.
3. Complete professional UAE legal review of terms, privacy, certificate, and liability language.
4. Define private document/evidence storage and retention controls.
5. Prepare pilot participation agreement or commercial MSA/DPA/SLA as appropriate.
6. Validate pricing with real buyer conversations.
7. Define billing and account lifecycle process.
8. Test support and dispute escalation with real or controlled cases.
9. Prepare first ESG report from real transfer evidence.
10. Re-score commercial and governance readiness before paid launch.

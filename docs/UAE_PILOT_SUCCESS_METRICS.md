## Pilot Pass Criteria

Wave 1 passes if:

- At least 5 organizations complete guided onboarding.
- At least 5 real or founder-accepted controlled handovers are completed.
- Certificates are generated for 100% of completed handovers.
- No Critical security, verification, certificate, or cross-tenant issues remain open.
- Average satisfaction is at least 4/5, or the founder explicitly accepts a lower score with documented remediations.
- Participants understand Dashboard -> Discover -> Requests -> Transfers -> Impact without heavy explanation by the end of their first guided workflow.

Wave 1 is Conditional Pass if:

- Fewer than 5 transfers complete due to participant availability, but at least 2 complete workflows produce trustworthy evidence.
- No Critical issues occur.
- High issues have founder-approved workarounds.
- The founder can identify a clear remediation path before expanding.

Wave 1 fails if:

- Any unresolved Critical issue remains.
- Cross-organization data exposure occurs.
- Certificates cannot be generated or verified for completed transfers.
- Participants cannot complete the core workflow even with founder guidance.
- Trust, safety, or operational concerns make live expansion inappropriate.

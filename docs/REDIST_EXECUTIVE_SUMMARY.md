# ReDist Executive Summary

Date: 2026-06-21  
Status: Strategic partner and investor briefing framework  
Evidence level: Product, staging, documentation, and pilot-readiness evidence only. Real pilot results are not yet available.

## Problem

Organizations in the UAE regularly hold usable surplus resources that are difficult to redistribute in a controlled, trusted, and evidence-backed way.

Common operating pain points:

- Suppliers may have surplus inventory, furniture, food-adjacent supplies, packaging, equipment, or other reusable materials but lack a structured handover workflow.
- Recipient organizations may need resources but cannot easily discover verified available surplus.
- Existing coordination often happens through informal calls, messages, spreadsheets, and manual follow-up.
- Sustainability and ESG teams need credible evidence, not vague claims.
- Founders, operators, and administrators need visibility into requests, transfers, verification, certificates, and impact.

## Solution

ReDist is a UAE-first redistribution management platform for verified organizations.

It turns surplus redistribution into an operational workflow:

1. A supplier lists available surplus.
2. A recipient discovers the resource and submits a request.
3. The supplier reviews, approves, declines, or schedules the request.
4. Both parties complete a handover workflow.
5. ReDist records verification, certificate evidence, and impact metrics.

The product is intentionally positioned as an enterprise redistribution platform, not a consumer marketplace.

## Market

ReDist is initially focused on UAE organizations where operational surplus, ESG reporting, and trust requirements overlap.

Target sectors:

- Hotels and hospitality groups.
- Restaurants and food-service operators.
- Warehouses and logistics operators.
- Retail and procurement-heavy organizations.
- NGOs, charities, and recipient networks.
- Corporate ESG and sustainability teams.
- Government-related ecosystem partners.

Assumption: UAE sustainability priorities, food waste reduction initiatives, hospitality density, and ESG reporting pressure create a favorable early market. This assumption requires validation through live outreach and pilot participation.

## Impact

ReDist is designed to track:

- AED value recovered from redistributed resources.
- Items redistributed.
- Waste diverted from disposal or idle storage.
- Transfer evidence through certificates.
- Recipient-side usefulness and social impact.
- Environmental impact estimates where defensible data exists.

Important boundary: ReDist does not currently have completed real-world pilot impact results. All external impact claims must be limited to product capability, simulation readiness, or clearly labeled estimates until Wave 1 produces approved evidence.

## Business Model

The recommended commercial model is subscription-led:

- Free founder-guided pilot tier for early validation.
- Starter organization tier for small verified organizations.
- Professional organization tier for repeat usage and reporting.
- Enterprise tier for multi-location groups, governance, support, and reporting.
- ESG reporting package for certificate-backed executive reporting.

ReDist should avoid transaction fees, payments, escrow, or success-fee claims until legal, operational, and compliance review is complete.

# ReDist Hiring Roadmap

Date: 2026-06-22  
Status: Lean hiring plan for UAE-first growth  
Principle: Hire only when repeated operational work is blocking founder focus or pilot quality.

## Hiring Philosophy

ReDist should avoid building a heavy team before real pilot evidence exists.

Hire when:

- Work repeats across multiple organizations.
- Founder response time becomes a pilot risk.
- Trust, verification, or support quality would suffer without ownership.
- Revenue, sponsorship, or funding can support the role.

Do not hire for:

- Unvalidated future scale.
- Product ideas that are not pilot blockers.
- Roles that can be handled through process during Wave 1.

## First Hire: Operations & Customer Success Lead

Recommended timing:

- After 2-5 active pilot organizations, or sooner if founder cannot maintain daily follow-up.

Why first:

- Virtual Pilot Wave 1 identified founder capacity as a risk.
- Early ReDist success depends on guided execution, not feature volume.
- This role can absorb operations, onboarding, support, tracking, and first-line issue triage.

Core responsibilities:

- Maintain daily operations runbook.
- Update pilot tracking center and issue log.
- Coordinate supplier/recipient handovers.
- Run onboarding scripts.
- Prepare weekly review notes.
- Escalate verification, trust, and technical issues.

Hiring trigger:

- Founder spends more than 8-10 hours/week on repetitive operations and support, or more than 3 active organizations require weekly hand-holding.

## Second Hire: Verification Reviewer

Recommended timing:

- After 10-15 organizations are in pipeline or verification reviews exceed founder capacity.

Why second:

- Trust depends on verification discipline.
- Founder should not personally review every routine document forever.
- A reviewer can prepare decisions while founder retains final authority for risky cases.

Core responsibilities:

- Organization profile review.
- Document checklist review.
- Category eligibility review.
- Certificate consistency checks.
- Trust review preparation.

Hiring trigger:

- More than 5 verification reviews/week, or any active backlog older than 2 business days.

## Third Hire: Customer Success Specialist

Recommended timing:

- After 15-25 active organizations or recurring support tickets become predictable.

Why third:

- Separate customer success from operations once support volume grows.
- Protect onboarding quality and participant satisfaction.
- Reduce founder involvement in routine user questions.

Core responsibilities:

- Supplier onboarding.
- Recipient onboarding.
- Support questions.
- Feedback collection.
- Training materials.
- Meeting follow-up.

Hiring trigger:

- More than 20 support tickets/month, more than 6 onboarding calls/month, or repeated confusion across multiple organizations.

## Later Roles

### Trust & Safety Lead

Timing:

- After recurring disputes, restricted category questions, or certificate challenges emerge.

Trigger:

- More than 2 disputes/month, any Critical trust incident, or regulated category expansion.

### Dedicated Engineering Owner

Timing:

- Required if technical incidents, deployment needs, or workflow bugs cannot be handled quickly by the current owner.

Trigger:

- More than 2 critical technical issues/quarter, active customer-impacting bugs, or enterprise security requirements.

## Recommended First Hire Profile

Title:

- Operations & Customer Success Lead.

Ideal background:

- UAE operations, hospitality, logistics, nonprofit, or customer success experience.
- Strong written follow-up.
- Comfortable with checklists, trackers, and structured escalation.
- Calm with suppliers and recipients.
- Understands that certificates and impact metrics must not be overclaimed.

First 30-day success criteria:

- Takes over daily tracker updates.
- Runs onboarding calls using scripts.
- Owns follow-up queue.
- Keeps issue log current.
- Produces weekly operations summary.
- Escalates unclear trust/category issues to founder.

Status: Public supplier page copy draft  
Audience: UAE organizations with surplus resources

## Hero

Headline:

Subheadline:

CTA:

## Who This Page Is For

ReDist is for UAE organizations that may have usable surplus, including:

- Hotels and hospitality groups.
- Restaurants and food-service operators.
- Warehouses and logistics operators.
- Retail and procurement-heavy organizations.
- Corporate facilities and operations teams.
- Event, training, or inventory-heavy organizations.

## Supplier Problem

Surplus is often useful, but hard to move responsibly.

Supplier questions:

- Who can use this?
- Is the recipient organization legitimate?
- How do we approve the request?
- Who handles handover?
- What evidence exists after completion?
- Can this support sustainability reporting?

## Supplier Value Proposition

ReDist helps suppliers:

- Make surplus visible to verified organizations.
- Keep approval control before handover.
- Track requests and transfer status.
- Coordinate practical handover details.
- Generate certificate-backed completion evidence.
- Build a clearer record of recovered value and redistribution impact.

## What Suppliers Can List

Pilot-safe examples may include:

- Furniture.
- Fixtures.
- Reusable warehouse materials.
- Cartons and packaging.
- Storage bins.
- Non-sensitive equipment.
- Packaged non-perishable supplies.
- Other low-risk reusable resources.

Category note:

Prepared food, medical items, hazardous goods, regulated goods, or legally sensitive categories require separate review and may not be accepted for early pilot workflows.

## Supplier Workflow

1. Confirm organization eligibility.
2. Create a surplus listing.
3. Receive requests from verified recipients.
4. Approve, decline, or clarify requests.
5. Coordinate handover.
6. Verify completion.
7. Review certificate and impact evidence.

## Trust and Control

ReDist is not an open public marketplace.

Suppliers keep control over:

- What gets listed.
- Which requests are approved.
- What quantity is released.
- When the resource is ready for handover.
- Whether a workflow should proceed.

## Pilot Invitation

ReDist is preparing a controlled founder-guided UAE pilot.

The ideal first supplier:

- Has one low-risk surplus category.
- Can assign one responsible contact.
- Can complete one workflow within two weeks.
- Is willing to provide direct feedback.
- Understands that pilot evidence must be reviewed before public claims.

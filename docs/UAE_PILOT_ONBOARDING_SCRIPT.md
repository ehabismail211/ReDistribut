# UAE Pilot Onboarding Script

Date: 2026-06-21  
Pilot mode: Founder-guided UAE Pilot Wave 1  
Audience: First supplier and recipient organizations

## Purpose

Use this script to onboard the first UAE Pilot Wave 1 participants consistently without turning the call into a sales pitch or product demo.

The goal is to help the participant understand what to do first, what ReDist is validating, and how to provide useful feedback.

## 5-Minute Onboarding Call

Use when the participant already understands the pilot and only needs orientation.

### Minute 0-1: Welcome

Hi [Name], thank you for joining.

This is a private founder-guided pilot for ReDist. The goal is to validate a real redistribution workflow between verified UAE organizations: surplus listing, resource request, handover, certificate, and impact evidence.

### Minute 1-2: Role

For this pilot, your organization is joining as:

- Supplier: you have surplus inventory to list.
- Recipient: you need resources and will submit a request.
- Mixed: you may do both, but we will focus on one first workflow.

Today we will focus only on your first guided step.

### Minute 2-4: First Action

Supplier first action:

- Sign in.
- Open Dashboard.
- Create one surplus listing.
- Confirm quantity, condition, location, urgency, and handover readiness.

Recipient first action:

- Sign in.
- Open Dashboard.
- Go to Discover.
- Review available surplus.
- Submit one request if a resource is suitable.

### Minute 4-5: Feedback

Please tell me immediately if anything is confusing, missing, or feels risky. After the first workflow, I will ask you to complete a short feedback form.

## 10-Minute Onboarding Call

Use for most first participant calls.

### Minute 0-1: Welcome And Scope

Thank you for joining ReDist's first UAE Pilot Wave 1.

This is a private, founder-guided pilot. It is not a public launch and not a marketplace. We are testing whether verified organizations can redistribute surplus resources with clear requests, handover verification, certificates, and impact evidence.

### Minute 1-3: Pilot Purpose

We are validating four things:

1. Can your organization understand what action to take today?
2. Can suppliers list surplus clearly?
3. Can recipients request resources clearly?
4. Can both sides trust the handover, certificate, and impact record?

### Minute 3-5: Workflow Overview

The workflow is:

Dashboard -> Discover -> Requests -> Transfers -> Impact

Supplier mindset:

- I have surplus inventory.
- I create/manage a listing.
- I review incoming requests.
- I verify handover completion.

Recipient mindset:

- I need resources.
- I discover available surplus.
- I submit a request.
- I track request and transfer status.
- I review certificate evidence.

### Minute 5-8: First Guided Step

Supplier:

- We will create one listing.
- We will confirm category, quantity, condition, city, and handover details.
- We will prepare for the first recipient request.

Recipient:

- We will review Discover.
- We will assess available resource details.
- We will submit one request only if the resource is suitable.

### Minute 8-10: Confidentiality And Feedback

Please keep the staging URL, screenshots, login details, and organization data private.

Feedback is part of the pilot. I will ask you about ease of use, trust, clarity, problems, suggestions, and overall rating.

## 20-Minute Onboarding Session

Use when the participant is new to ReDist or when the founder wants to observe the full first step.

### Minute 0-2: Welcome

Thank you for joining the ReDist UAE Pilot Wave 1.

This session is founder-guided. I will walk you through the workflow and observe where the product is clear or confusing.

### Minute 2-4: What ReDist Is

ReDist helps verified organizations redistribute surplus resources through:

- Available surplus listings.
- Recipient requests.
- Supplier review.
- Handover verification.
- QR-verifiable transfer certificates.
- Impact evidence.

This pilot is focused on operational clarity and trust, not public scale.

### Minute 4-6: What ReDist Is Not Yet

ReDist is not yet:

- A public marketplace.
- A payment or invoicing system.
- A replacement for your internal safety/compliance process.
- A self-serve launch for unrestricted organizations.
- A workflow for restricted, hazardous, medical, pharmaceutical, or controlled goods.

### Minute 6-8: Participant Role

Confirm:

- Organization name.
- Participant name and role.
- Supplier, recipient, or mixed role.
- Resource category.
- City/location.
- Support contact.
- Any safety or confidentiality concerns.

### Minute 8-14: Guided Product Walkthrough

Open the protected staging URL:

`https://re-distribut-web-git-main-ehab3.vercel.app`

Review:

1. Dashboard: what should I do today?
2. Discover: what resources are available?
3. Requests: what needs action?
4. Transfers: what handovers need verification?
5. Impact: what value was created?

### Minute 14-18: First Action

Supplier:

- Create or review one listing.
- Confirm quantity and handover readiness.
- Explain what incoming requests should look like.

Recipient:

- Find a suitable resource.
- Review practical handover information.
- Submit or prepare first request.

### Minute 18-20: Close And Next Step

Confirm:

- What step was completed.
- What was confusing.
- What the participant needs next.
- When the first transfer/handover should happen.
- When feedback will be submitted.

## Founder Observation Prompts

During the call, watch for:

- Does the participant know where to start?
- Do they understand the difference between Discover, Requests, and Transfers?
- Do they trust the supplier/recipient information shown?
- Do they understand certificate and impact evidence?
- Are there missing details needed for a real handover?
- Does mobile access matter for their workflow?

## Closing Script

Please treat the staging environment and pilot details as private. I will record your feedback, update the pilot tracker, and follow up with the next step.

The next step is: [next step].

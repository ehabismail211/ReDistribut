# ReDist Executive Deck

Purpose: 10-15 slide founder meeting presentation for executives, ESG leaders, hotel GMs, warehouse leaders, NGO leadership, and strategic partners.  
Recommended duration: 20-25 minutes plus 10 minutes discussion.  
Brand assets: use `apps/web/public/brand/redistribut-wordmark.svg` on slide 1 and final slide; use `apps/web/public/brand/redistribut-mark.svg` as a small footer mark.  
Website QR destination: `https://www.redistribut.com`

## Brand And Layout Rules

- Use ReDist brand-900 `#102A2A` for titles.
- Use brand-700 `#1F5C55` for primary accents and section labels.
- Use brand-50 `#F2FBF7` or white backgrounds.
- Use sand-500 `#C6A15B` only for UAE Pilot Edition highlights.
- Keep cards at 8px radius and use subtle borders.
- Do not use shopping, cart, buyer/seller, charity-only, or overclaimed ESG language.
- QR code placement: bottom-right on title and final slides; footer-size QR on appendix slides.
- Image placeholders should use real product screenshots, website captures, or neutral operational photos after approval.

## Slide 2: Executive Summary

Layout: two-column summary with three proof cards.

Key message:

ReDist is a UAE-first redistribution management platform for verified organizations. It helps suppliers list usable surplus, recipients request resources, and both parties complete controlled handovers with verification, certificate evidence, and impact visibility.

Cards:

- Organization-to-organization workflow.
- Founder-guided UAE pilot.
- Evidence-first impact visibility.

## Slide 3: The Problem

Layout: left problem narrative, right friction list.

Message:

Useful surplus exists, but redistribution is often handled through scattered calls, messages, spreadsheets, and informal contacts.

Friction:

- Unknown recipient suitability.
- Incomplete resource and request details.
- Unclear handover responsibility.
- Missing evidence after completion.
- ESG teams need proof, not screenshots.

## Slide 5: The ReDist Solution

Layout: large workflow ribbon.

Workflow:

List -> Discover -> Request -> Approve -> Handover -> Verify -> Certificate -> Impact

Callout:

Not a consumer marketplace. ReDist is a controlled redistribution workflow for verified organizations.

## Slide 6: How It Works

Layout: seven step cards with owner labels.

1. Supplier lists approved surplus.
2. Recipient discovers resources.
3. Recipient submits request.
4. Supplier approves, declines, or clarifies.
5. Both parties coordinate handover.
6. Completion is verified.
7. Certificate and impact evidence are reviewed.

Image placeholder: workspace dashboard/discover screenshot.

## Slide 7: Trust And Verification

Layout: trust stack with icon cards.

Trust controls:

- Founder-guided pilot access.
- Organization-level participation.
- Supplier approval before release.
- Verification where required.
- Handover confirmation.
- Certificate-backed operational evidence.
- Issue logging and founder review.

Boundary note:

Certificates are operational evidence, not legal guarantees or audited ESG certificates.

## Slide 8: Impact And ESG

Layout: metric cards with evidence note.

Designed to track:

- AED value recovered.
- Items redistributed.
- Waste diverted.
- Certificate evidence.
- Recipient usefulness.
- Environmental estimates where defensible.

Evidence rule:

No public impact claims until real transfers are completed, verified, and approved for publication.

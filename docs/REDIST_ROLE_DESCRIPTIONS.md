# ReDist Role Descriptions

Date: 2026-06-22  
Status: V1 role descriptions for founder-operated pilot to UAE growth  
Scope: Lean team, minimum viable operations.

## Role: Founder / General Manager

Reports to: Company ownership / board when applicable.  
Direct reports: Operations Lead, Engineering Owner, future commercial lead.

Responsibilities:

- Own company strategy and UAE pilot direction.
- Approve pilot participants and high-risk categories.
- Lead strategic partnerships and commercial conversations.
- Make final Go / Conditional Go / No-Go decisions.
- Own legal, governance, investor, and reputation decisions.
- Review first real transfer post-mortems.

KPIs:

- First real transfer completed safely.
- Qualified supplier and recipient pipeline.
- No Critical trust, safety, or data-boundary incidents.
- Partner conversations completed.
- Clear Continue / Expand / Pause decisions.

## Role: Operations Lead

Reports to: Founder.

Responsibilities:

- Run daily operations.
- Maintain pilot trackers, issue logs, and success metrics.
- Coordinate suppliers and recipients.
- Monitor Requests and Transfers queues.
- Confirm handover readiness.
- Prepare weekly operations reviews.
- Escalate Critical/High issues.

KPIs:

- Daily tracker updated.
- Active transfers have clear next action.
- Critical issues escalated same day.
- High issues assigned within 1 business day.
- Onboarding and handover follow-ups completed on time.
- Founder time spent on routine ops decreases.

## Role: Verification Reviewer

Reports to: Operations Lead. Founder retains final authority for exceptions.

Responsibilities:

- Review organization profiles and documents.
- Confirm authorized contact and category eligibility.
- Review document expiry and relevance.
- Prepare approval, rejection, or request-changes recommendation.
- Check certificate data against completed transfers.
- Maintain verification notes.

KPIs:

- Verification reviews completed within 2 business days.
- No approved organization missing required pilot evidence.
- Restricted category questions escalated.
- Certificate mismatches flagged before external sharing.

## Role: Customer Success

Reports to: Operations Lead.

Responsibilities:

- Run supplier and recipient onboarding.
- Explain listing, request, handover, certificate, and impact workflows.
- Capture user confusion and feedback.
- Maintain support scripts and FAQ updates.
- Triage support tickets.
- Help organizations complete first workflow.

KPIs:

- Onboarding calls completed on schedule.
- Support first response within agreed target.
- Feedback captured within 48 hours after workflow.
- Repeated confusion points logged.
- First workflow completion rate improves.

## Role: Trust & Safety

Reports to: Founder initially, then Operations Lead with founder escalation.

Responsibilities:

- Review disputes and misuse reports.
- Investigate misleading listings or unsafe categories.
- Maintain restricted category controls.
- Handle certificate disputes.
- Coordinate incident response for trust and safety events.
- Preserve evidence and escalation records.

KPIs:

- Critical incidents paused immediately.
- Disputes logged and assigned same day.
- Restricted category decisions documented.
- Certificate disputes resolved or escalated.
- No unresolved High trust issue older than target.

## Role: Engineering Owner

Reports to: Founder.

Responsibilities:

- Maintain app stability, deployment, and validation.
- Fix critical pilot blockers.
- Protect secrets and environment configuration.
- Monitor access control and data-boundary risks.
- Support staging and production incidents.
- Review technical feasibility of real pilot blockers.

KPIs:

- Tests and validation remain passing.
- Critical technical blockers resolved quickly.
- No secrets committed.
- Access control issues handled immediately.
- Deployments remain reproducible and documented.

## Reporting Principles

- Operations Lead owns execution.
- Founder owns final risk decisions.
- Engineering Owner owns technical integrity.
- Verification Reviewer and Customer Success should not independently approve risky categories.
- Trust & Safety can pause workflows pending founder review.

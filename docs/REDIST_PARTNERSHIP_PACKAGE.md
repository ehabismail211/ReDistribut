# ReDist Partnership Package

Date: 2026-06-21  
Status: Strategic partnership framework  
Evidence level: Partner-ready narrative and pilot asks. No live partner results yet.

## Partnership Goal

ReDist is seeking strategic partners who can help validate and scale trusted surplus redistribution in the UAE.

Best-fit partners can provide one or more of:

- Supplier organizations with usable surplus.
- Recipient organizations with real resource needs.
- ESG or sustainability guidance.
- Pilot sponsorship.
- Operational feedback.
- Trust, compliance, or ecosystem introductions.

## NGO Partnerships

Partner role:

- Validate recipient workflows.
- Identify acceptable resource categories.
- Review certificate usefulness.
- Provide feedback on trust, clarity, and handover burden.

ReDist value:

- Easier discovery of available resources.
- Structured request and handover process.
- Certificate-backed transfer evidence.
- Impact summaries for internal or donor reporting.

Initial ask:

- Participate in one founder-guided workflow.
- Review one certificate.
- Identify any trust, eligibility, or documentation gaps.

## Hotel Partnerships

Partner role:

- Validate supplier workflows for hospitality surplus.
- Provide real operational feedback on listings, requests, and handovers.
- Identify multi-location needs for future enterprise workflows.

ReDist value:

- Controlled surplus listing process.
- Approval control before any handover.
- Evidence for sustainability and internal reporting.
- Potential reduction in informal coordination.

Initial ask:

- Select one low-risk surplus category.
- Assign one operations or sustainability owner.
- Complete one guided workflow with an approved recipient.

## Restaurant Partnerships

Partner role:

- Validate surplus workflows in a time-sensitive operating environment.
- Identify safe pilot categories and compliance considerations.
- Provide practical feedback on speed, clarity, and responsibility.

ReDist value:

- Structured redistribution workflow.
- Request and approval control.
- Certificate-backed evidence.
- Potential sustainability story after real evidence exists.

Initial ask:

- Start with a founder-approved low-risk category.
- Avoid sensitive or regulated workflows until reviewed.
- Complete one request-to-handover test with founder support.

## Corporate ESG Partnerships

Partner role:

- Sponsor or support pilot workflows.
- Introduce supplier and recipient organizations.
- Review executive impact reporting needs.
- Help define credible evidence standards.

ReDist value:

- Practical circular economy execution platform.
- Certificate-backed transfer records.
- Pilot impact snapshots.
- Future ESG reporting package potential.

Initial ask:

- Sponsor one controlled pilot cohort or introduce qualified participants.
- Review impact model and reporting disclaimers.
- Identify buyer requirements for future commercial adoption.

## Government Collaboration Concepts

Potential collaboration areas:

- Circular economy ecosystem learning.
- Waste reduction pilot alignment.
- Organization verification and trust model feedback.
- Sector-specific guidance for safe redistribution categories.
- Introductions to qualified organizations.

Important boundary:

ReDist should not imply government endorsement, approval, or certification unless formally granted in writing.

## Top Partnership Targets

Priority targets for first conversations:

1. UAE Food Bank: recipient ecosystem guidance and compliant workflow learning.
2. ne'ma: food loss and waste ecosystem guidance.
3. UAE Restaurant Group: restaurant introductions and sector feedback.
4. Replate UAE: ecosystem learning and enterprise workflow comparison.
5. Sustainability-forward hotel groups: hospitality surplus and ESG reporting validation.
6. Warehouse/logistics operators: bulk reusable materials and transfer proof.
7. Corporate ESG sponsors: funding, introductions, and reporting feedback.

## Partner Qualification Criteria

Invite partners when they can:

- Assign a responsible contact.
- Support one controlled workflow or high-quality feedback session.
- Work with low-risk pilot categories.
- Respect staging access and confidentiality.
- Provide direct feedback within 48 hours.

Do not invite partners when they require:

- Public self-serve access before validation.
- Regulated or high-risk categories as a first workflow.
- Legal or audited ESG guarantees that ReDist does not yet provide.
- Unrestricted access to other organizations' data.

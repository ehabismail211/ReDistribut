# ReDist UAE Pilot Partnership One-Pager

Date: 2026-06-21  
Audience: UAE organizations, ecosystem partners, NGOs, hospitality groups, warehouses, and sustainability leaders

## Single-Page Overview

ReDist is a UAE-first circular economy platform that helps verified organizations redistribute surplus resources through controlled workflows, trust signals, handover verification, impact tracking, and QR-verifiable transfer certificates.

The current pilot is private and founder-guided. It is designed to validate real organization-to-organization redistribution workflows before wider launch.

## Mission

ReDist's mission is to make surplus redistribution trustworthy, auditable, and operationally practical for UAE organizations.

We help organizations move usable surplus from where it is not needed to where it can create value, while preserving evidence of the transfer and the resulting impact.

## Pilot Objectives

The UAE Pilot Wave 1 objectives are:

- Validate supplier surplus listing workflows.
- Validate recipient resource discovery and request workflows.
- Complete guided handovers between verified organizations.
- Generate transfer certificates for completed handovers.
- Review credible impact evidence.
- Identify operational, trust, and UX improvements before expansion.

## Who We Are Looking For

Wave 1 is looking for:

- Restaurant or food operators with safe surplus.
- Hotels with furniture, fixtures, or operating supplies.
- Warehouses or distributors with bulk packaging or materials.
- NGOs, charities, schools, or community organizations with real resource needs.
- Ecosystem partners who can advise on safe, compliant redistribution.

## Benefits

### For Suppliers

- Reduce usable surplus waste.
- Connect with approved recipient demand.
- Track request and handover status.
- Generate certificate-backed transfer evidence.
- Build impact records from completed redistribution.

### For Recipients

- Discover available resources from approved suppliers.
- Submit clear requests.
- Track approval and transfer status.
- Receive certificate-backed proof after completed handovers.
- Review impact evidence.

### For Partners

- Support UAE circular economy objectives.
- Help define safe redistribution standards.
- Learn what real organizations need before scale.
- Support a controlled, evidence-based pilot.

## Pilot Commitment

Each pilot organization should expect:

- 10-20 minute intro call.
- Guided onboarding.
- One real listing or request.
- One assisted handover where possible.
- Certificate and impact review.
- Short feedback form.

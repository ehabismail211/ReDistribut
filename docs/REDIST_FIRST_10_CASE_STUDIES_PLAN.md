# ReDist First 10 Case Studies Plan

Date: 2026-06-22  
Status: Future evidence plan  
Evidence boundary: This is a target plan, not a record of completed results.

## Purpose

This plan defines the first 10 case study categories ReDist should aim to capture after real transfers begin.

Goal:

- Build a balanced evidence base across supplier types, recipient types, resource categories, and impact objectives.

## Target Case Study Portfolio

| Case Study | Category | Supplier Type | Recipient Type | Impact Objective | Evidence Requirements |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| CS-01 | Furniture | Hotel / office / hospitality | Community center / school | Prove low-risk first handover | Condition notes, photos, certificate, feedback |
| CS-02 | Warehouse stock | Warehouse / logistics | NGO / training center | Prove bulk quantity workflow | Quantity records, pickup notes, certificate |
| CS-03 | Supplies | Hotel / corporate office | NGO / community organization | Prove useful everyday surplus | Listing, request, feedback, certificate |
| CS-04 | Equipment | Corporate / hospitality | Training center | Prove equipment suitability workflow | Condition notes, suitability feedback |
| CS-05 | Packaging materials | Warehouse / retail | Small organization / NGO | Prove reusable materials workflow | Quantity, pickup readiness, certificate |
| CS-06 | Fixtures | Hotel / retail | Community facility | Prove renovation surplus workflow | Photos, condition, handover proof |
| CS-07 | Restaurant non-food surplus | Restaurant group | NGO / community organization | Prove restaurant workflow without food risk | Category approval, request, feedback |
| CS-08 | Food-adjacent packaged supplies | Restaurant / food service | Approved recipient | Prove cautious food-adjacent workflow | Compliance review, handling notes, certificate wording |
| CS-09 | Multi-location supplier | Hotel / enterprise group | Recipient organization | Prove enterprise expansion potential | Branch details, approval flow, evidence |
| CS-10 | Partner-facilitated transfer | ESG sponsor / NGO partner | Supplier + recipient pair | Prove partner channel value | Partner referral record, workflow evidence |

## Desired Partner Types

Supplier-side:

- Hotel group.
- Warehouse/logistics operator.
- Restaurant group.
- Corporate office/facilities team.
- Retail/procurement-heavy organization.

Recipient-side:

- NGO.
- Community support organization.
- School or training center.
- Social enterprise.
- Approved operational recipient.

Partner-side:

- ESG sponsor.
- Food redistribution ecosystem partner.
- Hospitality network.
- Logistics partner.
- Corporate sustainability team.

## Impact Objectives

Across the first 10 case studies, ReDist should seek evidence for:

- Value recovered.
- Items redistributed.
- Waste diverted.
- Recipient usefulness.
- Certificate usefulness.
- Supplier operational control.
- Handover clarity.
- Partner referral value.
- Commercial willingness signal.

## Evidence Requirements

Every case study requires:

- Completed transfer.
- Listing screenshot or reference.
- Request reference.
- Transfer reference.
- Certificate reference.
- Supplier feedback.
- Recipient feedback.
- Founder observations.
- Approval for any public claims.

Optional evidence:

- Photos.
- Approved quotes.
- Public logo use.
- Impact estimate notes.
- Partner referral record.

## Recommended First Case Study Target

First case study target:

- Category: Furniture.
- Supplier type: Hotel or hospitality organization.
- Recipient type: Community center, school, or NGO.
- Resource: Chairs, shelving, tables, or fixtures.

Why:

- Lower compliance risk than food.
- Practical handover evidence.
- Strong visual story potential.
- Clear recipient usefulness.
- Strong fit with the virtual pilot recommendation.

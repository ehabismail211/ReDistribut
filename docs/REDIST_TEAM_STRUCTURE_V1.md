# ReDist Team Structure V1

Date: 2026-06-22  
Status: Lean UAE-first team design  
Purpose: Define the minimum team required to scale ReDist from founder-operated pilot to UAE growth stage.

## Operating Principle

ReDist should stay founder-led until real workflows prove repeatability. Hiring should follow operational load, not ambition.

The first team structure should minimize fixed cost while protecting:

- Participant trust.
- Verification discipline.
- Transfer reliability.
- Certificate integrity.
- Founder focus.
- Product stability.

## V1 Team Structure

| Role | Stage Needed | Primary Purpose | Employment Model |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Founder | Now | Strategy, pilot governance, commercial decisions, final escalation | Full-time founder |
| Operations Lead | First hire | Own daily pilot execution, workflow follow-up, issue routing | Full-time or fractional-to-full-time |
| Verification Reviewer | Second or fractional support | Review organizations, documents, category eligibility, certificate evidence | Fractional first |
| Customer Success | Third hire or combined with Operations Lead | Onboarding, support, participant training, feedback capture | Combined early, separate later |
| Trust & Safety | Later or founder-owned initially | Disputes, restricted categories, misuse, escalations | Founder-owned, then fractional |
| Engineering Owner | Now | Deployment, security, data integrity, incidents, technical fixes | Founder/contractor/technical owner |

## Founder

Responsibilities:

- Decide pilot scope and category boundaries.
- Approve first live participants.
- Own strategic partnerships and commercial positioning.
- Handle high-risk trust, legal, ESG, and reputation decisions.
- Review the first real transfer post-mortem.
- Decide Continue, Expand, Pause, or Commercialize.

Founder should not permanently own:

- Every support reply.
- Every tracking center update.
- Every meeting follow-up.
- Routine verification checklist work.
- Repetitive onboarding walkthroughs.

## Operations Lead

Purpose:

The Operations Lead turns founder-guided execution into a repeatable operating system.

Responsibilities:

- Run daily operations checklist.
- Track listings, requests, transfers, certificates, and issues.
- Coordinate supplier and recipient next steps.
- Prepare onboarding sessions.
- Maintain pipeline and pilot trackers.
- Escalate Critical/High issues.

This is the recommended first hire.

## Verification Reviewer

Purpose:

Protect trust and eligibility while keeping founder decisions focused on exceptions.

Responsibilities:

- Review organization identity and contact details.
- Check submitted documents against verification checklist.
- Confirm category eligibility.
- Flag restricted or unclear categories.
- Review certificate data for consistency.
- Maintain verification notes.

Early model:

- Founder owns final approval.
- Reviewer prepares recommendations.

## Customer Success

Purpose:

Help suppliers and recipients complete workflows without founder hand-holding.

Responsibilities:

- Run onboarding calls.
- Answer supplier and recipient questions.
- Explain certificate and impact limits.
- Capture feedback.
- Maintain help scripts and support logs.
- Identify repeated confusion points.

Early model:

- Combined with Operations Lead until support volume justifies a separate role.

## Trust & Safety

Purpose:

Handle sensitive workflows, disputes, misuse, and category risk.

Responsibilities:

- Review disputes.
- Enforce restricted category rules.
- Investigate unsafe or misleading listings.
- Handle certificate disputes.
- Escalate legal, privacy, and reputation concerns.
- Maintain incident and risk records.

Early model:

- Founder owns Trust & Safety decisions.
- Operations Lead gathers facts.
- Legal/professional review is required for regulated categories.

## Engineering Owner

Purpose:

Keep ReDist stable, secure, and deployable while the pilot runs.

Responsibilities:

- Maintain staging and production deployments.
- Fix critical workflow bugs.
- Monitor validation failures.
- Protect secrets and environment configuration.
- Review access control and data boundary issues.
- Support incident response.

Early model:

- Founder or contracted technical owner.
- No feature development unless a real pilot blocker requires it.

## Reporting Structure

Founder-led stage:

```text
Founder
├── Engineering Owner
└── Operations Lead
    ├── Verification Reviewer
    ├── Customer Success
    └── Trust & Safety support
```

Early growth stage:

```text
Founder / GM
├── Operations Lead
│   ├── Verification Reviewer
│   ├── Customer Success
│   └── Trust & Safety
└── Engineering Owner
```

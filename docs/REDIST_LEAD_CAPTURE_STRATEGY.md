# ReDist Lead Capture Strategy

Date: 2026-06-22  
Status: Public website lead capture plan  
Scope: Contact forms, demo requests, partnership requests, pilot interest

## Lead Capture Principle

ReDist should capture enough information to qualify leads without creating a long enterprise procurement form.

The form should make clear:

- ReDist is in controlled pilot preparation.
- Access is not automatic.
- Founder review is required.
- High-risk categories may be declined or deferred.

## Contact Forms

Recommended primary form:

| Field | Required | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Full name | Yes | Contact owner |
| Work email | Yes | Avoid anonymous inquiries where possible |
| Organization name | Yes | Qualification required |
| Role / title | Yes | Understand authority and fit |
| City / emirate | Yes | Handover geography |
| Interest type | Yes | Supplier, recipient, partner, demo, ESG, other |
| Organization type | Yes | Hotel, restaurant, warehouse, NGO, corporate, other |
| Resource category or need | Yes | Short description |
| Timeline | No | Immediate, 2 weeks, 1 month, later |
| Message | No | Free text |
| Consent to contact | Yes | Required |

## Demo Requests

Demo CTA:

- Request founder walkthrough.

Demo qualification questions:

- Are you exploring as a supplier, recipient, or partner?
- What workflow do you want to understand?
- Are you considering pilot participation?
- Who else should join the demo?

Demo routing:

- Qualified supplier or recipient -> founder discovery call.
- Strategic partner -> partnership conversation.
- Investor/media/general -> founder review before scheduling.

## Partnership Requests

Partnership form intent:

- NGO partnership.
- Hotel or restaurant group.
- Warehouse or logistics operator.
- Corporate ESG sponsor.
- Government-related or ecosystem collaboration.
- Other.

Required prompt:

```text
How could your organization support or participate in a controlled ReDist pilot?
```

Routing:

- Add to [Partnership Pipeline](REDIST_PARTNERSHIP_PIPELINE.md).
- Use [Partner Meeting Template](REDIST_PARTNER_MEETING_TEMPLATE.md).

## Pilot Interest

Pilot interest CTA:

- Apply for pilot consideration.

Qualification questions:

- Do you have surplus resources or a resource need?
- What category is involved?
- Can your organization assign one responsible contact?
- Can you complete a guided workflow within two weeks?
- Are you comfortable providing feedback within 48 hours?

Pilot routing:

- Supplier lead -> supplier qualification.
- Recipient lead -> recipient qualification.
- Both sides needed -> hold until matched.
- High-risk category -> defer or reject until reviewed.

## Response Time Targets

| Lead Type | Target Response |
| --- | --- |
| Qualified pilot supplier | 1 business day |
| Qualified pilot recipient | 1 business day |
| Strategic partner | 2 business days |
| Investor / sponsor | 2 business days |
| General inquiry | 3 business days |
| High-risk category | Respond with caution and defer |

## Privacy and Consent Notes

Before public launch:

- Review contact form wording with legal/privacy advisor.
- Link to privacy framework or formal privacy policy when available.
- Do not collect sensitive documents through a basic marketing form.
- Do not request passwords, confidential files, financial details, or regulated data.

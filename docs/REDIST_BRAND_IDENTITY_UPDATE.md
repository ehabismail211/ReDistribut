# ReDist Brand Identity Update

Date: 2026-06-22  
Status: Initial refreshed identity assets

## Brand Idea

ReDist should feel like trusted redistribution infrastructure: practical, verified, and impact-aware. The logo should not look like a generic marketplace icon. It should communicate that surplus moves from one organization to another through a controlled, verified handover.

## Logo Concept

The refreshed mark uses:

- A circular redistribution loop to show resources moving from supplier to recipient.
- Two side nodes to represent organization-to-organization exchange.
- A central verification check to represent trust, handover completion, and evidence.
- UAE-relevant green tones to suggest sustainability, operational trust, and circular economy value.

## Usage Guidance

Use the mark when space is limited, such as favicon, app icon, social preview, cards, and workspace badges.

Use the wordmark when the audience needs to learn the brand, such as the website header, presentations, manuals, partner one-pagers, and sales material.

Do not distort, recolor, rotate, or place the logo on low-contrast backgrounds.

## Brand Message

Short line:

> Verified surplus redistribution.

Expanded line:

> ReDist helps verified organizations move surplus resources through request, handover, certificate, and impact workflows.

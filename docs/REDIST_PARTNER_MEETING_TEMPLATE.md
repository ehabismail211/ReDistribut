## Opening Positioning

Use this short framing:

```text
ReDist is a UAE-first platform for verified organizations to redistribute surplus resources through a controlled workflow: listing, discovery, request, approval, handover, verification, certificate, and impact evidence.

We are not publicly launching yet. We are speaking with a small number of UAE organizations to validate whether the workflow is useful, trustworthy, and operationally practical.
```

## Discovery Questions

### Organization Context

- What does your organization do in the UAE?
- Who manages surplus, donations, procurement, sustainability, or recipient coordination today?
- What resource categories are most relevant to your operations?
- How often do surplus or resource-need situations happen?
- What currently happens when surplus exists or resources are needed?

### Workflow Pain

- What is manual, unclear, or time-consuming today?
- Where do handovers break down?
- What information is usually missing?
- Who needs to approve or verify a transfer?
- What documentation is required after a handover?

### Trust and Risk

- What would make you trust another organization in a redistribution workflow?
- What categories should be avoided in an early pilot?
- What compliance, safety, or reputation concerns would block participation?
- What evidence would you need before accepting or providing resources?
- Who internally would need to approve a pilot?

## Pilot Questions

- Could your organization participate as a supplier, recipient, partner, or advisor?
- Is there one low-risk workflow that could be tested in the next 2 weeks?
- Can you assign one responsible contact for the pilot?
- What would make the first workflow successful?
- What would make it a failure?
- Would certificate-backed transfer evidence be useful?
- Would the Impact page help internal reporting or stakeholder communication?

## Segment-Specific Questions

### NGO / Recipient

- What resource categories would be genuinely useful?
- How do you verify that received resources match expectations?
- Would a certificate help with donors, internal reporting, or partner communication?
- What recipient eligibility or documentation is required?

### Hotel / Restaurant Supplier

- What surplus categories are safe and practical for a first workflow?
- How quickly does surplus need to move?
- Who approves release of surplus resources?
- What evidence would operations or sustainability teams want after completion?

### Warehouse / Logistics

- What reusable materials are commonly idle or discarded?
- Are quantity, pickup readiness, and location the biggest constraints?
- Who coordinates pickup or handover today?
- Would transfer proof reduce manual follow-up?

### Corporate ESG Partner

- What impact evidence would be credible internally?
- What metrics matter most: value recovered, items redistributed, waste diverted, CO2 estimate, recipient impact, or certificates?
- Would your organization sponsor or introduce a controlled pilot cohort?
- What claims must ReDist avoid until audited or legally reviewed?

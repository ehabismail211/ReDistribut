## Purpose

This tracking center is the live operating table for the first real UAE organizations. Update it after every founder touchpoint, onboarding step, workflow milestone, and feedback session.

Wave 1 is approved for founder-guided execution only. Do not use this tracker as approval for unmanaged public invites.

## Operating Rules

- Update this file after every participant session.
- Do not mark a workflow step Complete unless the founder has verified it.
- Log every confusion point or blocker in `docs/UAE_PILOT_ISSUE_LOG.md`.
- Do not expand beyond the first two organizations until the first supplier-recipient workflow is understood.
- Every completed transfer must have a certificate status recorded.
- Feedback is required from every participant before Wave 1 is closed.

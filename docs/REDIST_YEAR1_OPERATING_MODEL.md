# ReDist Year 1 Operating Model

Date: 2026-06-22  
Status: UAE-first operating model for founder-led pilot through early scale  
Scope: Lean startup model; minimize hiring until real volume demands it.

## Year 1 Objective

ReDist's Year 1 objective is to prove that UAE organizations can complete trusted, certificate-backed redistribution workflows with enough repeatability to justify commercial growth.

Year 1 should not optimize for headcount. It should optimize for:

- Safe first transfers.
- Clear operating evidence.
- Repeatable onboarding.
- Trusted verification.
- Honest impact reporting.
- Commercial learning.

## Stage 1: Founder-Only Stage

Timing:

- Current stage through first real transfer.

Team:

- Founder.
- Engineering Owner as available.

Operating model:

- One active transfer at a time.
- Founder qualifies every participant.
- Founder runs onboarding.
- Founder reviews certificates and impact.
- Founder maintains issue log and post-mortem.

Success criteria:

- First real supplier and recipient selected.
- First low-risk transfer completed.
- Certificate reviewed.
- Feedback collected.
- Post-mortem completed.

No-Go:

- Running multiple live transfers in parallel.
- High-risk categories.
- Public launch.

## Stage 2: Pilot Stage

Timing:

- After first transfer review through 2-5 active organizations.

Team:

- Founder.
- Operations & Customer Success Lead if founder workload exceeds threshold.
- Engineering Owner for technical validation and incidents.

Operating model:

- Founder still approves participants and categories.
- Operations support can maintain trackers and follow-up.
- One to two active transfers at a time.
- Weekly pilot review.

Success criteria:

- 2-3 verified transfers.
- No Critical trust or data incidents.
- Repeatable onboarding script.
- Clear top support issues.
- First commercial willingness signal.

Hiring trigger:

- Founder spends more than 8-10 hours/week on repetitive operations.

## Stage 3: Early Growth Stage

Timing:

- 6-25 active organizations or recurring monthly transfers.

Team:

- Founder / GM.
- Operations Lead.
- Verification Reviewer.
- Customer Success as needed.
- Engineering Owner.

Operating model:

- Operations Lead owns daily workflow health.
- Founder handles partnerships, commercial, exceptions, and governance.
- Verification Reviewer prepares routine decisions.
- Customer Success supports onboarding and first workflow completion.
- Trust & Safety remains founder-led for escalations.

Success criteria:

- 10+ completed transfers.
- Support process is predictable.
- Verification backlog stays under 2 business days.
- At least one paying, sponsored, or strong commercial pathway emerges.

Hiring trigger:

- 20+ support tickets/month or 5+ verification reviews/week.

## Stage 4: UAE Scale Stage

Timing:

- 26-50+ active organizations, repeat transfers, and active commercial accounts.

Team:

- Founder / GM.
- Operations Lead.
- Customer Success.
- Verification Reviewer.
- Trust & Safety support.
- Engineering Owner.
- Commercial support if revenue pipeline justifies it.

Operating model:

- Founder leads strategy, enterprise relationships, governance, and financing.
- Operations team handles daily workflows.
- Trust & Safety handles disputes and restricted categories.
- Engineering supports reliability, security, and deployment.
- Customer Success handles onboarding, support, and retention.

Success criteria:

- Repeatable monthly transfer volume.
- Customer satisfaction evidence.
- No unresolved High/Critical trust issues.
- Commercial packaging validated.
- Legal and privacy review completed.

No-Go:

- Scaling into regulated categories without expert review.
- Claiming audited ESG outcomes without methodology and verification.
- Expanding faster than support and verification capacity.

## Year 1 Team Plan

| Quarter / Stage | Team Model | Hiring Decision |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Current | Founder-only | No hire yet; prepare role scorecards |
| First real transfer | Founder-only with technical support | No hire unless founder capacity blocks pilot |
| 2-5 organizations | Founder + Operations Lead | First hire likely needed |
| 6-15 organizations | Operations Lead + fractional verification | Add Verification Reviewer if backlog appears |
| 16-25 organizations | Operations Lead + Customer Success + verification | Add Customer Success if support volume repeats |
| 26+ organizations | UAE growth team | Add Trust & Safety and dedicated engineering capacity |

## Year 1 Organizational Readiness

Current organizational readiness: 72 / 100.

Target after first operations hire:

- 82 / 100 if an Operations & Customer Success Lead takes over routine follow-up, onboarding, and tracking.

Target after first 3 real transfers:

- 86 / 100 if support load is measured, issue logs are clean, and no Critical trust incidents occur.

## Recommended Year 1 Operating Constraint

Until the first real transfer post-mortem is complete:

- One supplier.
- One recipient.
- One low-risk resource.
- One founder-guided transfer.
- One certificate.
- One post-mortem.

Scale after evidence, not before.

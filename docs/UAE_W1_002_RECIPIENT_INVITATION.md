## Welcome Message

Hi [Contact Name],

Thank you for considering participation in ReDist's first UAE Pilot Wave 1.

ReDist helps verified organizations discover available surplus resources, submit requests, complete handovers, and receive certificate-backed impact evidence.

We would like to invite [Organization Name] as the first recipient participant in a small controlled pilot. The goal is to validate whether a real recipient organization can discover available resources, submit a clear request, complete a guided handover, and understand the resulting certificate and impact record.

This is a private founder-guided pilot, not a public self-serve launch.

## Pilot Purpose

The pilot will help validate:

- Whether recipients can quickly understand what resources are available.
- Whether filters and resource details support real request decisions.
- Whether request submission is clear enough for supplier review.
- Whether handover and verification steps are understandable.
- Whether certificates and impact evidence are useful after a completed transfer.

## Expected Workflow

During the founder-guided pilot, your team will:

1. Join a short onboarding call.
2. Confirm the type of resources your organization can use.
3. Access the protected ReDist staging workspace.
4. Review the Dashboard action center.
5. Use Discover to find a suitable available resource.
6. Submit one request with quantity and intended use.
7. Track the request status.
8. Coordinate a guided handover if approved.
9. Confirm receipt and transfer verification.
10. Review the generated certificate and QR verification path.
11. Review the impact summary.
12. Submit brief feedback.

## Feedback Expectations

We will ask for feedback on:

- Ease of discovering available resources.
- Whether resource details were complete enough.
- Whether request submission was clear.
- Trust and confidence in supplier/handover information.
- Whether status tracking made sense.
- Certificate usefulness.
- Impact reporting clarity.
- Any problems, missing information, or suggestions.
- Whether your organization would continue using ReDist after the pilot.

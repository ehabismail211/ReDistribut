## ReDist

### English

**Redistribute surplus resources with trust, evidence, and impact.**

ReDist is a UAE-first redistribution management platform for verified organizations. It helps suppliers list usable surplus, recipients request resources, and both parties coordinate handovers with verification, certificate evidence, and impact visibility.

### العربية

**إعادة توزيع الموارد الفائضة بثقة وإثبات وأثر.**

ReDist منصة إماراتية لإدارة إعادة التوزيع بين المؤسسات الموثقة. تساعد الموردين على عرض الفائض القابل للاستخدام، والمستفيدين على طلب الموارد، والطرفين على تنسيق التسليم مع التحقق وإثبات الشهادة ووضوح الأثر.

## Why It Matters

### English

Useful resources are often wasted or left idle because organizations lack a trusted workflow for discovery, approval, handover, and evidence. ReDist turns informal coordination into a structured operating process.

### العربية

تهدر الموارد المفيدة أو تبقى خاملة لأن المؤسسات لا تملك مسارا موثوقا للاكتشاف والموافقة والتسليم والإثبات. تحول ReDist التنسيق غير الرسمي إلى عملية تشغيلية منظمة.

## What ReDist Does

| Workflow | Outcome |
| --- | --- |
| List surplus | Suppliers make approved resources visible. |
| Discover resources | Recipients find suitable available surplus. |
| Request and approve | Both sides keep control before handover. |
| Transfer and verify | Completion is tracked and confirmed. |
| Certificate and impact | Evidence supports internal reporting and future ESG conversations. |

## Who It Serves

- Suppliers: hotels, restaurants, warehouses, offices, retailers, operators.
- Recipients: NGOs, schools, community organizations, training centers.
- Partners: ESG teams, ecosystem programs, sponsors, business councils.

## Pilot Focus

The UAE Pilot Wave 1 is founder-guided and controlled:

- One supplier.
- One recipient.
- One low-risk resource category.
- One handover.
- One certificate.
- One feedback cycle.

No public impact claims will be made until real transfers are completed, verified, and approved for publication.

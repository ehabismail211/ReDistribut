# UAE Pilot Training Guide

Date: 2026-06-20

Status: Ready for controlled pilot training after final live staging verification.

## Purpose

This guide trains the first UAE pilot users on how to use ReDist during the controlled pilot.

Target organizations:

- Restaurant A
- Restaurant B
- Hotel A
- Warehouse A
- NGO A

## Training Format

Recommended session length: 45-60 minutes.

| Segment | Duration | Owner |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Pilot purpose and expectations | 10 minutes | Founder |
| Login, role, and organization setup | 10 minutes | Founder |
| Verification and trust | 10 minutes | Founder |
| Listing/request workflow | 15 minutes | Founder + user |
| Handover, certificate, and feedback | 10 minutes | Founder |
| Questions and next action | 5 minutes | Founder |

## Pre-Training Gate

Before training an external organization:

1. Confirm `./.tools/pnpm staging:validate` passes.
2. Confirm invited account passwords have been rotated.
3. Confirm the user has the correct role: Organization Admin or Organization User.
4. Confirm the organization understands this is a controlled UAE pilot, not a public launch.
5. Confirm the founder or pilot coordinator is available during the first workflow.

## Training Objectives

By the end of training, users should know how to:

1. Access the ReDist pilot workspace.
2. Understand their organization role.
3. Complete organization setup.
4. Submit or understand verification requirements.
5. Publish a listing or create a request.
6. Accept or complete a request when authorized.
7. Review transfer certificate and QR verification.
8. Submit feedback or report issues.

## Role Guidance

| Role | Training Focus |
| --- | --- |
| Organization Admin | Organization setup, verification, listing approval, handover approval, feedback |
| Organization User | Discovery, requests, handover participation, feedback |
| Founder | Monitoring, verification review, issue triage, weekly reviews |

## Workflow Training

### Sender Workflow

1. Confirm resource is eligible for the pilot.
2. Publish listing with accurate quantity, category, location, and handover method.
3. Review incoming request.
4. Accept request only if quantity is available.
5. Coordinate handover.
6. Complete transaction.
7. Review certificate.
8. Submit feedback.

### Receiver Workflow

1. Discover available resources.
2. Confirm category and location fit.
3. Submit request with quantity and message.
4. Coordinate handover.
5. Confirm receipt.
6. Review certificate and impact summary.
7. Submit feedback.

## Verification Training

Explain:

- Why verification matters.
- Which documents are required.
- How verification affects trust and category eligibility.
- Why restricted categories need founder approval.

## Certificate Training

Explain:

- Certificates prove a recorded ReDist transfer.
- QR verification shows public-safe transfer details.
- Certificates are not tax invoices, payment receipts, customs documents, or legal title documents.

## Feedback Training

Feedback is required after the first completed transfer.

Users should report:

- What was confusing.
- What slowed them down.
- What they expected but could not do.
- Any bug or data issue.
- Whether the workflow was valuable enough to repeat.

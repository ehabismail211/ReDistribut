## Call Goal

If any target replies, book a 20-minute call.

### Call Agenda

1. Explain ReDist in 60 seconds.
2. Confirm the target's role in the ecosystem.
3. Ask what real surplus workflows look like today.
4. Ask what blocks redistribution.
5. Ask what proof or reporting matters.
6. Ask for one pilot-fit introduction.
7. Agree next step.

## Questions To Ask

| Question | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| What surplus workflows already happen informally? | Understand existing behavior |
| What makes organizations hesitant to redistribute? | Discover trust and compliance blockers |
| What proof is needed after handover? | Validate certificate value |
| Which categories are safest for a first pilot? | Reduce launch risk |
| Who should be involved in approval? | Identify real decision maker |
| Can you introduce one organization willing to test? | Convert ecosystem contact into pilot candidate |

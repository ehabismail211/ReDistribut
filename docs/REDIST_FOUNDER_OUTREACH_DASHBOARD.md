## Section 7: Top Risks

| Risk | Severity | Early Signal | Mitigation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| No response from target organizations | High | Fewer than 2 replies after 10 contacts | Use warm introductions and replace weak targets |
| Interest but no responsible owner | High | Meetings end without named owner | Require owner before pilot invitation |
| Use case is legally or operationally sensitive | High | First proposed category is regulated or high-risk | Defer category and choose low-risk workflow |
| Organizations want public launch guarantees | Medium | Prospect asks for public access, SLA, or contract before pilot | Reframe as founder-guided validation only |
| ESG claims misunderstood | Medium | Prospect asks whether certificate is audited ESG proof | Explain certificate is operational evidence, not audit |
| Recipient demand does not match supplier surplus | Medium | Good supplier but no recipient fit | Use NGO and ecosystem partners for matching |
| Founder time becomes bottleneck | Medium | More meetings than follow-up capacity | Limit active pilot invites to two organizations |
| Product feature requests distract from pilot | Medium | Prospects request non-critical features | Capture backlog; build only critical blockers |

## Section 8: Weekly Founder Actions

Every week during outreach:

1. Send 10 targeted first-contact messages.
2. Send all due follow-ups.
3. Book at least two discovery calls.
4. Complete at least one partner meeting.
5. Update [Partnership Pipeline](REDIST_PARTNERSHIP_PIPELINE.md).
6. Score every completed meeting using [Partner Meeting Template](REDIST_PARTNER_MEETING_TEMPLATE.md).
7. Move unresponsive prospects to `No response`.
8. Replace weak targets with warm referrals.
9. Review pilot risk in [UAE Pilot Issue Log](UAE_PILOT_ISSUE_LOG.md).
10. Decide whether any organization is ready for [Supplier Invitation](UAE_W1_001_SUPPLIER_INVITATION.md) or [Recipient Invitation](UAE_W1_002_RECIPIENT_INVITATION.md).

Weekly decision question:

```text
Do we have one qualified supplier, one qualified recipient, and one safe workflow that can complete within two weeks?
```

### Food / NGO Ecosystem

Subject: Controlled UAE pilot for auditable surplus redistribution

Hi [Name],

I’m the founder of ReDist, a UAE-first circular economy platform for verified organizations to redistribute surplus resources with trust, impact tracking, and QR-verifiable transfer certificates.

We have completed live staging validation and are now speaking with a small number of UAE organizations before onboarding the first real pilot users.

I would value 20 minutes of your guidance on compliant surplus food workflows, recipient validation, and what proof organizations need after a transfer.

Would you be open to a short call this week?

### Restaurant / Hotel

Subject: Invite-only UAE pilot for surplus transfer proof and impact tracking

Hi [Name],

I’m running a controlled UAE pilot for ReDist, a circular economy platform that helps verified organizations redistribute surplus resources and generate transfer certificates for completed handovers.

For restaurants and hotels, we are validating one guided workflow: list a real surplus item, match it with an approved recipient, complete the handover, and review the certificate and impact summary.

This is not a public marketplace launch. It is a small founder-led pilot to understand whether the workflow helps operations, sustainability, and reporting.

Would you be open to a 20-minute intro call?

## Stop Conditions

Do not invite a prospect if:

- First use case involves hazardous, medical, or legally sensitive resources.
- They require public launch terms or contractual guarantees before testing.
- They cannot assign a responsible user.
- They cannot provide feedback.
- They are asking for unmanaged marketplace access.

## Welcome Message

Hi [Contact Name],

Thank you for considering participation in ReDist's first UAE Pilot Wave 1.

ReDist is a protected, founder-guided platform for verified organizations to redistribute surplus resources with clear request workflows, handover verification, certificates, and impact evidence.

We would like to invite [Organization Name] as the first supplier participant in a small controlled pilot. The goal is to validate whether a real supplier can publish surplus inventory, respond to an approved recipient request, complete a guided handover, and review the resulting certificate and impact record.

This is not a public marketplace launch. It is a controlled pilot with founder support at every step.

## Pilot Purpose

The pilot will help validate:

- Whether surplus inventory can be listed clearly and safely.
- Whether recipient requests contain enough information for supplier decisions.
- Whether handover verification works in a real operating context.
- Whether transfer certificates and impact summaries are useful to suppliers.
- What information suppliers need before trusting a redistribution workflow.

## What You Will Do

During the founder-guided pilot, your team will:

1. Join a short onboarding call.
2. Confirm the type of surplus resource suitable for the pilot.
3. Access the protected ReDist staging workspace.
4. Create one surplus listing with quantity, condition, city, and handover details.
5. Review a recipient request.
6. Approve, decline, or request clarification.
7. Complete a guided handover if the request is accepted.
8. Confirm transfer verification.
9. Review the generated transfer certificate.
10. Review the impact summary.
11. Submit brief feedback.

## What Feedback Is Requested

We will ask for feedback on:

- Ease of creating a surplus listing.
- Whether request information was sufficient.
- Trust and confidence in the recipient workflow.
- Handover coordination clarity.
- Certificate usefulness.
- Impact reporting usefulness.
- Any missing information, confusing wording, or operational concerns.
- Whether your organization would continue using ReDist after the pilot.

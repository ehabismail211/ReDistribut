# ReDist Supplier Deck

Purpose: supplier-focused meeting presentation for hotels, restaurants, warehouses, distributors, retailers, office operators, and organizations with usable surplus.  
Slide count: 12.  
Recommended duration: 18-22 minutes plus 10 minutes discovery.  
QR destination: `https://www.redistribut.com`

## Deck Design

- Cover uses ReDist wordmark top-left and supplier-specific title.
- Use brand-700 for action labels: List, Review, Approve, Handover, Verify.
- Use operational screenshots where possible: listing form, requests queue, transfers, certificate.
- Place QR code on slides 1 and 12, bottom-right.
- Include Arabic speaker support note in speaker notes, not as crowded slide copy.

Title: ReDist For Suppliers  
Subtitle: Turn usable surplus into controlled redistribution with evidence.

## Slide 2: Supplier Problem

Layout: three pain columns.

Pain points:

- Surplus is handled through scattered messages.
- Recipient suitability is unclear.
- Handover evidence is difficult to preserve.

Speaker note:

Position ReDist as operational control, not charity pressure.

## Slide 3: Why Suppliers Use ReDist

Layout: value cards.

Supplier value:

- Keep approval control.
- Reduce waste and idle inventory.
- Review requests before release.
- Coordinate handover.
- Keep certificate evidence.
- Support ESG conversations with better records.

## Slide 4: Supplier Workflow

Layout: five-step flow.

List -> Review request -> Approve or clarify -> Coordinate handover -> Verify completion

Image placeholder: listing creation screenshot.

## Slide 5: What To List First

Layout: safe category matrix.

Good first categories:

- Furniture and fixtures.
- Hotel operating supplies.
- Packaging materials.
- Pallets, cartons, bins.
- Non-sensitive equipment.
- Packaged non-perishable supplies where suitable.

Needs separate review:

- Prepared food.
- Medical items.
- Hazardous goods.
- Regulated or unclear ownership goods.

## Slide 6: Request Review Control

Layout: request card mockup.

Supplier can review:

- Recipient organization.
- Quantity requested.
- Location and handover timing.
- Message and intended use.
- Approval, decline, or clarification path.

## Slide 7: Handover And Verification

Layout: handover checklist.

Handover should clarify:

- Pickup or delivery method.
- Location.
- Timing.
- Responsible contact.
- Completion confirmation.

## Slide 8: Certificate Evidence

Layout: certificate placeholder plus boundary note.

Certificate may include:

- Supplier.
- Recipient.
- Resource summary.
- Quantity.
- Transfer date.
- QR verification reference.

Boundary:

Operational evidence only; not legal, tax, safety, or audited ESG proof.

## Slide 9: Pilot Time Commitment

Layout: timeline with duration estimates.

- Intro call: 10-20 minutes.
- First login and orientation: 10 minutes.
- First listing: 10-15 minutes.
- Request review: 10-20 minutes.
- Certificate and feedback: 10 minutes.

## Slide 10: Supplier Qualification

Layout: pass/fail checklist.

Required:

- Usable surplus.
- Low-risk category.
- Decision maker.
- Coordinator.
- Clear quantity and condition.
- Practical handover location.
- Feedback readiness.

## Slide 11: Supplier FAQ

Layout: five Q&A tiles.

- Do we have to accept requests? No.
- Can we choose what to list? Yes, with founder approval for Wave 1.
- Is our organization public? Not without permission.
- Is ReDist responsible for item condition? No.
- What evidence do we receive? Transfer records and certificate evidence where supported.

## Problem Section

Headline:

Useful surplus should not disappear into informal coordination.

Body:

Many organizations hold resources that could be reused: furniture, supplies, warehouse materials, packaged goods, equipment, fixtures, and operational surplus.

But redistribution is often slowed by unanswered questions:

- Who needs this resource?
- Is the organization verified?
- What quantity is available?
- Who approves the request?
- When is handover ready?
- What evidence exists after completion?

ReDist gives suppliers, recipients, and administrators one operational workflow for redistribution.

## Trust Section

Headline:

Built around verified organizations and practical handover evidence.

Body:

ReDist is designed for organization-to-organization workflows, not anonymous public browsing.

Trust signals:

- Organization verification workflow.
- Role-based access.
- Request approval before handover.
- Transfer status tracking.
- Certificate-backed completion evidence.
- Impact metrics tied to completed workflows.

Important note:

ReDist certificates are operational evidence of a platform workflow. They are not legal guarantees, government approvals, safety certifications, tax receipts, or audited ESG certificates.

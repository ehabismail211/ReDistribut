# ReDist Partnership Meeting Pack

Audience: ESG teams, corporate partners, government-related entities, hotel groups, NGO networks, accelerators, business councils, sponsors, and ecosystem collaborators  
Use: Strategic partner discovery and qualification

## Partnership Opportunities

### English

ReDist partnerships can help create trusted redistribution infrastructure in the UAE. Partners may contribute supply networks, recipient networks, ESG alignment, pilot sponsorship, category expertise, visibility, or operational support.

### العربية

يمكن لشراكات ReDist أن تساعد في بناء بنية موثوقة لإعادة التوزيع في الإمارات. قد يساهم الشركاء بشبكات الموردين أو المستفيدين أو توافق ESG أو رعاية التجربة أو خبرة الفئات أو الظهور أو الدعم التشغيلي.

## ESG Alignment

### English

ReDist supports ESG conversations by helping organizations document completed redistribution activity:

- Waste diversion.
- Recovered value.
- Items redistributed.
- Transfer verification.
- Certificate references.
- Recipient usefulness.

Important: ReDist does not present operational certificates as audited ESG assurance.

### العربية

تدعم ReDist محادثات ESG عبر مساعدة المؤسسات على توثيق نشاط إعادة التوزيع المكتمل:

- تحويل الهدر.
- القيمة المستردة.
- العناصر المعاد توزيعها.
- التحقق من التحويل.
- مراجع الشهادات.
- فائدة المستفيد.

مهم: لا تقدم ReDist الشهادات التشغيلية كضمان ESG مدقق.

## Pilot Participation Options

| Option | Description | Best For |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Supplier partner | Bring organizations with usable surplus. | Hotels, warehouses, corporate offices |
| Recipient partner | Bring approved organizations with real needs. | NGOs, schools, community networks |
| ESG partner | Support evidence standards and reporting conversations. | Sustainability teams, ESG advisors |
| Ecosystem partner | Help ReDist reach qualified pilot participants. | Business councils, accelerators |
| Sponsor | Support pilot execution and future case studies. | Corporate CSR / impact sponsors |

## Strategic Partner Discovery Questions

1. What type of organizations are in your network?
2. Do you have access to suppliers, recipients, or both?
3. What ESG or circular economy goals matter most?
4. What would make a pilot credible to your stakeholders?
5. Would your organization participate directly or refer others?
6. Can you identify one safe first category?
7. Can you help validate evidence standards?
8. What public visibility would require approval?
9. What compliance review is needed before involvement?
10. What would make the partnership valuable in 90 days?
11. Who owns the decision internally?
12. Who would coordinate the relationship?

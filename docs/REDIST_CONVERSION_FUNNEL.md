# ReDist Conversion Funnel

Date: 2026-06-22  
Status: Marketing website conversion funnel plan  
Scope: Public website visitor to pilot participant

## Funnel Principle

The website should not optimize for raw signups. It should optimize for qualified founder conversations and safe pilot candidates.

## Funnel Stages

| Stage | Definition | Goal | Owner | Source / Destination |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Visitor | Person lands on public website | Understand ReDist and pick intent | Website | Analytics |
| Lead | Visitor submits form or requests call | Capture qualified inquiry | Founder / Operations | Contact form to pipeline |
| Meeting | Lead books or completes founder conversation | Qualify fit and risks | Founder | [Partner Meeting Template](REDIST_PARTNER_MEETING_TEMPLATE.md) |
| Pilot invite | Qualified lead receives controlled invitation | Confirm safe use case and responsible contact | Founder | [Supplier Invitation](UAE_W1_001_SUPPLIER_INVITATION.md) / [Recipient Invitation](UAE_W1_002_RECIPIENT_INVITATION.md) |
| Pilot participant | Organization receives protected access and onboarding | Complete guided workflow | Founder / Operations | [Pilot Tracking Center](UAE_PILOT_WAVE1_TRACKING_CENTER.md) |

## Funnel Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target Before Public Expansion |
| --- | --- | ---: |
| Visitor to lead conversion | Leads / website visitors | Establish baseline first |
| Lead qualification rate | Qualified leads / total leads | 20-40% |
| Meeting booking rate | Meetings booked / qualified leads | 50%+ |
| Pilot invite rate | Invites / completed meetings | 20-30% |
| Pilot activation rate | Active participants / invites | 50%+ |
| First workflow completion rate | Completed workflows / active pilot pairs | 50%+ during pilot |

## Qualification Criteria

A lead is qualified when:

- It represents a real organization.
- It has a responsible contact.
- It has a relevant surplus, need, or partnership role.
- It can support a low-risk pilot use case.
- It understands the founder-guided pilot model.
- It is willing to provide feedback.

Stop conditions:

- Hazardous, medical, regulated, or legally sensitive first use case.
- Request for public self-serve access.
- No responsible owner.
- No willingness to provide feedback.
- Expectation of audited ESG proof, government approval, delivery, escrow, or legal certification.

## Funnel Operating Rhythm

Daily:

- Review new leads.
- Route by intent.
- Respond to qualified inquiries.
- Log in pipeline.

Weekly:

- Review conversion by channel.
- Review lead quality.
- Update top objections.
- Decide whether CTAs or form fields need adjustment.

Monthly:

- Review lead-to-meeting conversion.
- Review meeting-to-pilot conversion.
- Review pilot participant quality.
- Decide whether to expand website traffic or keep outreach founder-led.

# ReDist Scaling Thresholds

Date: 2026-06-22  
Status: Team scaling trigger framework  
Scope: UAE-first pilot through early growth.

## Purpose

This document defines when ReDist should add team capacity based on real operating load.

Hiring should follow measured thresholds:

- Number of organizations.
- Number of transfers.
- Number of support tickets.
- Number of disputes.
- Verification backlog.
- Founder operating load.

## Organization Thresholds

| Active Organizations | Operating Model | Team Need |
| ---: | --- | --- |
| 1-2 | Founder-only guided pilot | No hire required |
| 3-5 | Founder plus part-time operations support | First hire trigger if workflows are active |
| 6-15 | Early pilot cohort | Operations Lead required |
| 16-25 | Early growth | Customer Success support likely needed |
| 26-50 | UAE growth stage | Operations Lead, Customer Success, Verification Reviewer |
| 50+ | UAE scale stage | Dedicated Trust & Safety and Engineering capacity |

## Transfer Thresholds

| Monthly Transfers | Operating Model | Team Need |
| ---: | --- | --- |
| 1-3 | Founder-guided | Founder can handle if low-risk |
| 4-10 | Repeat pilot operations | Operations Lead needed |
| 11-25 | Early growth | Operations Lead plus Customer Success |
| 26-50 | Operational scale | Verification Reviewer and Trust & Safety process needed |
| 50+ | Scale operations | Dedicated operations pod and technical monitoring |

## Support Ticket Thresholds

| Monthly Support Tickets | Risk | Team Need |
| ---: | --- | --- |
| 0-10 | Manageable | Founder or Operations Lead |
| 11-20 | Watch | Operations Lead should own support triage |
| 21-40 | High | Customer Success role needed |
| 41-75 | Very High | Customer Success plus structured help content |
| 75+ | Scale support | Dedicated support process and tooling |

## Dispute Thresholds

| Monthly Disputes | Risk | Team Need |
| ---: | --- | --- |
| 0 | Normal | Founder review only |
| 1 | Watch | Founder plus Operations Lead evidence collection |
| 2-3 | High | Trust & Safety support needed |
| 4+ | Critical | Pause expansion and review categories/process |

Dispute types:

- Request disputes.
- Transfer disputes.
- Condition disputes.
- Certificate disputes.
- Eligibility disputes.
- Misuse or restricted category reports.

## Verification Thresholds

| Weekly Verification Reviews | Team Need |
| ---: | --- |
| 0-3 | Founder can handle |
| 4-5 | Founder plus checklist support |
| 6-10 | Verification Reviewer needed |
| 11-20 | Reviewer plus founder exception process |
| 20+ | Dedicated verification process and tooling review |

Backlog rule:

- Any verification backlog older than 2 business days triggers additional support or reduced onboarding pace.

## Founder Time Thresholds

| Founder Hours / Week on Routine Ops | Assessment | Action |
| ---: | --- | --- |
| 0-5 | Healthy | Continue founder-led |
| 6-10 | Watch | Prepare first hire |
| 11-15 | High | Hire or assign Operations Lead |
| 16+ | Critical | Pause expansion until operating support exists |

Routine ops includes:

- Tracker updates.
- Follow-ups.
- Support replies.
- Onboarding scheduling.
- Verification checklist work.
- Handover reminders.
- Certificate explanation.

## Scaling Decision Table

| Trigger | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 2 active pilot organizations and first transfer pending | Keep founder-led |
| 3+ active organizations with weekly follow-up needs | Add Operations Lead |
| 5+ verification reviews/week | Add Verification Reviewer or fractional support |
| 20+ support tickets/month | Add Customer Success |
| 2+ disputes/month | Add Trust & Safety support |
| 2+ critical technical incidents/quarter | Add dedicated Engineering Owner |
| Any Critical trust/security incident | Pause expansion immediately |

## Recommended Current Stage

Current stage: Founder-only, preparing for Pilot Stage.

Recommended constraint:

- Do not run more than one real transfer in parallel until the first transfer post-mortem is complete.
- Do not invite more than 2-5 organizations before operations support is identified.

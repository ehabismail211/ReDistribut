## Pipeline Rules

- Keep this document updated after every message, call, and follow-up.
- Do not send protected staging access until the organization is qualified.
- Do not treat interest as pilot commitment until a responsible contact, safe use case, and next action are confirmed.
- Do not invite organizations whose first use case involves hazardous, medical, legally sensitive, or unreviewed regulated resources.
- Move unresponsive prospects to `No response` after two follow-ups and replace them with a new target.

Qualification rule:

- `22+ / 30`: Candidate for guided pilot invitation.
- `16-21 / 30`: Keep as partner or learning conversation.
- `0-15 / 30`: Do not invite yet.
- Any stop condition: Disqualify or defer, regardless of score.

## Stop Conditions

Do not invite if:

- First use case is hazardous, medical, regulated, or legally sensitive.
- They require production public access before validation.
- They cannot assign a responsible user.
- They cannot provide feedback.
- They ask for audited ESG guarantees or legal transfer certification.
- They expect ReDist to manage delivery, payments, escrow, or tax treatment.

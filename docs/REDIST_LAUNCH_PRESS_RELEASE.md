ReDist prepares UAE pilot for trusted surplus redistribution between verified organizations

## Body

ReDist, a UAE-first redistribution management platform, is preparing a controlled pilot to help verified organizations move usable surplus resources through a clearer, evidence-backed workflow.

The platform is designed for organization-to-organization redistribution, helping suppliers list available surplus, recipients request resources, and both parties coordinate handovers with operational evidence after completion.

The first founder-guided pilot will focus on a small number of UAE organizations and low-risk resource categories. The goal is to validate whether real suppliers and recipients can complete a practical workflow from listing to request, handover, certificate, and impact review.

"Useful surplus should not depend only on scattered messages and informal coordination," said [Founder Name], founder of ReDist. "ReDist is being built to give organizations a trusted way to move resources from surplus to need while preserving evidence of what happened."

ReDist is not launching as a public self-serve marketplace. Early access will be controlled, founder-guided, and focused on learning from real workflows before broader rollout.

The pilot will explore:

- How suppliers list and manage surplus resources.
- How recipient organizations discover and request resources.
- How handovers are coordinated and verified.
- How certificate-backed evidence supports trust.
- How impact metrics can be presented responsibly.

ReDist is initially focused on UAE sectors where surplus, operational coordination, and ESG reporting needs intersect, including hospitality, restaurants, warehouses, logistics, NGOs, community organizations, and corporate sustainability teams.

## Current Status

ReDist has completed internal product readiness, protected staging validation, pilot planning, governance frameworks, commercial planning, operations runbooks, outreach materials, and virtual pilot rehearsal.

Real pilot results are not yet available. ReDist will publish impact results only after completed workflows are verified and participants approve external use.

## About ReDist

ReDist is a UAE-first redistribution management platform for verified organizations. It helps organizations list surplus resources, discover available resources, submit requests, coordinate handovers, verify completion, generate certificate-backed evidence, and track impact visibility.

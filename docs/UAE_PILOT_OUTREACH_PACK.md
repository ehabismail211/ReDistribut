# UAE Pilot Outreach Pack

Date: 2026-06-20  
Status: Ready for founder outreach  
Mode: Controlled, invite-only UAE pilot

## Purpose

This pack helps the founder recruit the first real UAE pilot organizations after simulator-first validation.

Use it for:

- 2 Restaurants
- 1 Hotel
- 1 Warehouse
- 1 NGO

## Pilot Positioning

ReDist is a UAE-first circular economy platform that helps organizations redistribute surplus resources through verified, auditable, certificate-backed workflows.

This is not a charity marketplace. It is an enterprise operating layer for surplus visibility, trust, transfer control, and impact reporting.

## Who To Contact First

Prioritize organizations with:

- A clear surplus or resource need.
- A named operations owner.
- Willingness to complete one assisted transaction.
- Comfort using a staging pilot environment.
- Ability to give direct feedback within 48 hours.

Avoid for the first cohort:

- Highly regulated or hazardous resources.
- Organizations needing complex approvals before any test.
- Users who only want a public marketplace.
- Organizations that cannot assign a pilot contact.

## Short Founder Pitch

ReDist is running a controlled UAE pilot for organizations that want to reduce waste, recover value, and document resource transfers.

The pilot is invite-only. We guide your team through one real workflow: organization setup, verification, listing or request, handover, impact tracking, and a QR-verifiable transfer certificate.

The goal is simple: prove whether this helps your operations before we scale it.

## Intro Call Agenda

Time: 20 minutes

1. Confirm organization type and operating context.
2. Ask what surplus or resource needs exist today.
3. Explain controlled pilot scope.
4. Confirm likely first listing or request.
5. Confirm contact owner and preferred support channel.
6. Explain verification and certificate expectations.
7. Decide whether to invite them into the pilot.

## Qualification Questions

| Question | Why It Matters |
| --- | --- |
| What surplus resources do you commonly have or need? | Confirms real workflow fit |
| How often does this happen? | Tests repeat potential |
| Who approves giving or receiving items? | Identifies admin user |
| What documentation would you need internally? | Tests certificate value |
| Can you complete one guided workflow in the next 2 weeks? | Confirms pilot timing |
| Are there any regulated or sensitive categories involved? | Protects pilot scope |
| Who can provide feedback after the first transaction? | Ensures learning |

## Invitation Criteria

Invite the organization only if:

- They have a clear first use case.
- They can assign an owner/admin.
- They understand this is a controlled pilot.
- They can complete feedback within 48 hours.
- The first use case is not outside approved pilot categories.

Do not invite if:

- They require production guarantees beyond pilot scope.
- They want unmanaged public marketplace access.
- Their first use case depends on restricted categories without founder review.
- They cannot identify a responsible user.

## Supplier A: Dubai Marina Restaurant Group

Profile:

- Type: Multi-branch restaurant operator.
- Location: Dubai Marina and JLT.
- Responsible user: Operations Manager.
- Secondary user: Sustainability Coordinator.
- Motivation: Reduce usable surplus loss and create evidence for sustainability reporting.

Likely surplus:

- Prepared meals with short expiry.
- Sealed packaged food supplies.
- Event leftovers that meet safety conditions.
- Reusable packaging and service items.

Pilot-safe categories:

- Sealed packaged dry goods.
- Non-food service supplies.
- Prepared meals only if compliance and food-safety controls are confirmed.

Risk notes:

- Food handling and safety rules require professional review before real use.
- Time sensitivity may expose workflow friction.
- Certificate language must not imply legal food-safety certification.

## Supplier B: Abu Dhabi Hospitality Properties

Profile:

- Type: Hotel and serviced apartment group.
- Location: Abu Dhabi.
- Responsible user: Procurement Lead.
- Secondary user: Housekeeping Inventory Manager.
- Motivation: Reuse renovation surplus and support ESG reporting.

Likely surplus:

- Banquet chairs.
- Linen and towels.
- Small appliances.
- Guest-room fixtures.
- Event furniture.

Pilot-safe categories:

- Furniture.
- Fixtures.
- Non-sensitive operating supplies.
- Packaged non-perishable hospitality items.

Risk notes:

- Item condition must be described clearly.
- Pickup readiness and loading location matter.
- Multi-location ownership may require admin approval.

## Supplier C: Jebel Ali Warehouse Operator

Profile:

- Type: Warehouse and logistics operator.
- Location: Jebel Ali.
- Responsible user: Warehouse Manager.
- Secondary user: Compliance Coordinator.
- Motivation: Move surplus materials, avoid storage congestion, and track reuse evidence.

Likely surplus:

- Reusable plastic pallets.
- Cartons and packaging.
- Storage bins.
- Shelving components.
- Returned but usable handling materials.

Pilot-safe categories:

- Pallets.
- Empty cartons.
- Plastic bins.
- Non-hazardous warehouse supplies.

Risk notes:

- Quantity controls must prevent over-allocation.
- Pickup instructions need to be operationally precise.
- Bulk handovers may require truck access and scheduled windows.

- Location: Dubai / Sharjah.
- Responsible user: Program Coordinator.

- Motivation: Receive useful resources with reliable evidence and clear handover records.

Likely needs:

- Food-adjacent supplies.
- Packaged goods.
- Hygiene supplies.

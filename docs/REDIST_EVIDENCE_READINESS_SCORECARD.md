## Story Publication Gate

Publish only when:

- Transfer evidence score is at least 80.

- Publication approval score is at least 80.
- No unresolved High/Critical issue is attached to the transfer.

## Recommended First Evidence Target

Target:

- Furniture transfer from hotel/hospitality supplier to community recipient.

Success story category:

- Furniture.

Reason:

- Lower compliance risk.
- Clear visual evidence.
- Easy to describe condition and quantity.
- Strong recipient usefulness potential.
- Good fit for a certificate-backed handover story.

# UAE Supplier Sales Deck

Date: 2026-06-21  
Audience: Restaurants, hotels, warehouses, distributors, retailers, and suppliers with surplus resources  
Use: Founder-led intro calls before UAE Pilot Wave 1

## Slide 1: ReDist

ReDist helps verified UAE organizations redistribute surplus resources through a controlled workflow with trust, handover verification, impact tracking, and QR-verifiable transfer certificates.

This is not a public marketplace launch. The first pilot is private, founder-guided, and limited to approved organizations.

## Slide 2: The Problem

Many organizations have usable surplus but no simple, trusted way to move it responsibly.

Common supplier problems:

- Surplus inventory is handled manually through calls, WhatsApp, or ad hoc contacts.
- Teams do not know which recipients are trustworthy or suitable.
- Handover details are hard to track.
- Quantity, condition, and timing are often unclear.
- There is limited evidence after a transfer is completed.
- Sustainability and ESG teams need proof, not screenshots.
- Operations teams need a workflow that does not create extra risk.

## Slide 3: Why This Matters In The UAE

UAE organizations are increasingly expected to reduce waste, improve circular economy practices, and show credible impact.

But responsible redistribution requires:

- Verified organizations.
- Clear resource details.
- Permissioned requests.
- Safe categories.
- Handover confirmation.
- Certificate-backed evidence.
- Founder-guided learning before scale.

## Slide 4: The ReDist Solution

ReDist provides an operating workflow for redistribution:

1. Supplier lists approved surplus resources.
2. Recipient discovers available resources.
3. Recipient submits a request.
4. Supplier reviews and approves or declines.
5. Both sides complete a handover.
6. ReDist records verification.
7. A transfer certificate and impact summary are generated.

## Slide 5: What Suppliers Can List In The Pilot

Preferred first pilot categories:

- Prepared or packaged food surplus where safe and permitted.
- Furniture and fixtures.
- Hotel operating supplies.
- Packaging materials.
- Pallets, cartons, bins, and warehouse materials.
- Community-use supplies.

Excluded from first pilot unless separately approved:

- Medical or pharmaceutical items.
- Hazardous materials.
- Controlled goods.
- Items with unclear ownership.
- Goods requiring payment, tax, customs, or title-transfer workflows.

## Slide 10: FAQ

### Is this a marketplace?

No. ReDist includes resource discovery, but the core product is a controlled redistribution workflow for verified organizations.

### Is there a cost?

The first controlled pilot is focused on validation. Commercial terms are discussed only after the workflow proves useful.

### Do we need full verification before starting?

For safe pilot categories, founder-approved manual clearance may be used. Restricted or sensitive categories require stricter review.

### Can we choose what to list?

Yes, but the founder must approve the first pilot category to keep Wave 1 safe and manageable.

### Is the certificate a legal document?

No. It is platform proof of a recorded ReDist transfer, not a tax invoice, payment receipt, customs document, or legal title document.

### Can our organization name be kept private?

Yes. ReDist will not publish your organization name, inventory details, certificate data, or feedback publicly without permission.

### What if the handover fails?

The issue is logged, the workflow is paused if needed, and the founder helps decide whether to retry, close, or defer.

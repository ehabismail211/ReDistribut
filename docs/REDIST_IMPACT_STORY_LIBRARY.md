# ReDist Impact Story Library

Date: 2026-06-22  
Status: Story category library for future real evidence  
Evidence boundary: Categories are prepared for future stories. No real results are claimed here.

## Purpose

This library defines the impact story categories ReDist should be ready to capture after real transfers are completed.

Each story should be connected to:

- Completed transfer evidence.
- Certificate reference.
- Participant feedback.
- Approved quotes or anonymized summaries.
- Clearly labeled impact estimates.

## Food

Current status:

- High-potential category, but sensitive for first pilot.
- Prepared food or time-sensitive food workflows require compliance review.

Potential story angle:

- "How a verified supplier moved safe surplus food-adjacent resources to a recipient organization with clear handover evidence."

Evidence required:

- Food/category eligibility approval.
- Handling notes.
- Supplier and recipient confirmation.
- Certificate language reviewed for safety overclaim risk.
- Feedback from both parties.

Publication caution:

- Do not imply food safety certification.
- Do not publish beneficiary counts unless provided and approved.

## Furniture

Current status:

- Strong first case study candidate.
- Lower compliance risk than food-adjacent categories.

Potential story angle:

- "How surplus furniture from a hospitality or office environment supported a community organization."

Evidence required:

- Condition notes.
- Photos with consent.
- Quantity transferred.
- Recipient usefulness feedback.
- Certificate reference.

Recommended first success story category:

- Furniture.

## Equipment

Current status:

- Good category if equipment is non-sensitive, safe, and condition is clear.

Potential story angle:

- "How reusable equipment avoided idle storage and supported recipient operations."

Evidence required:

- Equipment condition.
- Suitability notes.
- Handover confirmation.
- Any safety disclaimers.
- Certificate reference.

Publication caution:

- Avoid regulated, medical, hazardous, or safety-critical equipment unless reviewed.

## Supplies

Current status:

- Strong category for hospitality, offices, schools, and community programs.

Potential story angle:

- "How surplus supplies moved from storage to useful community deployment."

Evidence required:

- Quantity.
- Condition or package status.
- Recipient intended use.
- Handover evidence.
- Feedback form.

## Warehouse Stock

Current status:

- Strong operational category for early B2B evidence.

Potential story angle:

- "How reusable warehouse materials moved through a verified request and handover process."

Examples:

- Pallets.
- Cartons.
- Storage bins.
- Shelving.
- Packaging materials.

Evidence required:

- Quantity.
- Pickup readiness.
- Vehicle/loading notes.
- Verification record.
- Certificate reference.

## Community Support

Current status:

- Good umbrella category for stories involving NGOs, training centers, schools, and community organizations.

Potential story angle:

- "How a recipient organization used ReDist to source practical resources for its operations."

Evidence required:

- Recipient approval.
- Usefulness feedback.
- Optional beneficiary context if provided and approved.
- Certificate reference.
- Photos with consent.

Publication caution:

- Do not infer social impact beyond what the recipient approves.

## Story Prioritization

Recommended first story order:

1. Furniture.
2. Warehouse stock.
3. Supplies.
4. Equipment.
5. Community support.
6. Food, only after compliance review.
